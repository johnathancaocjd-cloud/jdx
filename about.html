ABOUT JDX

A daily US market briefing — published before the open.

JDX delivers a concise, well-sourced read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts. Three editions are published every morning at 9am HKT, so you can pick how deep you want to go.

What's in each edition

Every briefing covers the same core ground — macro & Fed, geopolitics, earnings, and US large-cap movers. The three editions differ in depth, not topic.

EditionLengthAdds over previous
Concise2-3 pages · 5 minHeadlines, top movers, today's catalysts
Standard4-6 pages · 10 minSector rotation, watchlist scan, pre-market futures
In-depth7-10 pages · 20 minTechnicals, options, analyst ratings, week-ahead

How it's made

Each morning, an automated pipeline gathers data from public sources — exchange data, Fed releases, earnings reports, news wires — and produces three briefings sized for different reading depths. The briefings are written in a finance-news editorial voice, with sources cited at the bottom of every page.

Why three editions

Most morning briefings make you choose between superficial (too little to act on) and exhaustive (no time to read). JDX gives you the choice — same writers, same data, three depths. Skim the Concise edition over coffee; read the In-depth on the days you're actually trading.
