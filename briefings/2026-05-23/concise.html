TL;DR

The US markets saw a positive session, with the S&P 500 rising +0.37% to 7,473.47 and the Nasdaq 100 increasing +0.42% to 29,481.64. The Dow Jones also gained +0.58% to 50,579.70.

Tech and financials led the charge, with AAPL rising +1.26% and JPM gaining +1.12%. The VIX fell -0.36% to 16.70, indicating a decrease in market volatility.
