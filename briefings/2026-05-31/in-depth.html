The US market rose on the back of a strong tech performance, with the MSFT and AVGO leading the charge. The VIX fell to 15.32, indicating a decrease in market volatility. Our editorial take is that the market is due for a correction, but the current trend is still bullish.

Overall, the market is looking strong, but we need to keep an eye on the VIX and the IWM for any signs of weakness.
